what was the inspiration?


well, The "Dawn of the Quan" is a very Unique
and Mystyque Project by far.

The inspiration of this project came out of my own
life experiences. some of the elements in this story
is based on facts and true events.

This started out as a personal memoir of Memorable events that transpired in my life, whether it was good, bad or ugly. among the roster of events, some of those events were very strange.
I experienced what some would call paranormal, outerworldy, or supernatural encounters;While on my personal Journey of evolution, self discovery and Re-Discovery.
that in itself though very turbulent to say the least,
it has been beyond extraordinary.
So I asked myself one day, how can I write a memoir ,
because no one is gonna believe me,  
in most minds it would be Facts vs Fiction.

Well I realized even in my own life,
that truth is stranger than fiction.  
With all that being said... This is Where it all began.
The happenings in my life became the inspiration for this project among others.
this unique Story (My~Story) has evolved into a scifi master piece.
