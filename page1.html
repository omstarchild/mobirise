synopsis

An intangible and silent war between mankind and human beings. Homo~sapiens (from the Earth Plane) vs Homo~aureus (The golden people of the sun).
In this Fallen 3RD Dimensional world, everyone looks like the same. species .We have been taught there is no difference, but there is.
There's nothing more intriguing than a mysterious situation, where you're thrown into the deep end, but what if that mysterious situation was erased from your memory?
My name is Egyn,
and I just recently learned I am a solar plex being living a human Experience here on this plane they call a planet.
I am the off spring/decendant of the first solar plex beings visiting this planet, I am homo~aureus. We are the children of the sun.  
it started with memories of a realities, dimensions, and other worlds . I began to questioned my current  reality and the nature of my existence.
I discovered those in high places ,that I trusted lied to me, about my true Nature and origin. This was to control me others like myself to keep us isolated from our species outside of this planet.
I stopped at nothing to unravel the mystery of my true identity.        But as for my exact place of origin , that is another quest. because  something on the inside is telling me, I never had an origin ,
because I don't remember a time I never was...
